# Privacy Policy — Sheet Show: The Idle Spreadsheet Game

**Effective date:** April 6, 2026

This privacy policy applies to the Android application **Sheet Show: The Idle Spreadsheet Game** ("the App"), developed by **Dillon** ("we", "us", or "our").

---

## 1. Information We Collect

We do not collect, store, or transmit any personally identifiable information directly.

All game progress (businesses, money, prestige level, settings) is saved **locally on your device only** using Android's SharedPreferences system. This data never leaves your device and is not accessible to us.

---

## 2. Third-Party Services

The App uses the following third-party services that may collect data independently:

### Google AdMob
We display ads through Google AdMob. AdMob may collect device identifiers and usage data to serve personalized or non-personalized ads, depending on your device settings and consent preferences.

- [Google Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy)
- [How Google uses data from apps](https://policies.google.com/technologies/partner-sites)

You can opt out of personalized advertising through your Android device settings under **Settings → Google → Ads → Opt out of Ads Personalization**.

### Google Play Billing (In-App Purchases)
The App offers optional in-app purchases processed through Google Play. We do not handle or store any payment information. All transactions are managed by Google Play.

- [Google Play Terms of Service](https://play.google.com/about/play-terms/)

---

## 3. Data We Do Not Collect

- We do not require account creation or login
- We do not collect your name, email, phone number, or location
- We do not use analytics SDKs or crash reporting tools
- We do not share any data with third parties beyond those listed above

---

## 4. Children's Privacy

The App is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided personal information through a third-party service used by the App, please contact that service directly.

---

## 5. Data Retention

Since all game data is stored locally on your device, you can delete it at any time by uninstalling the App.

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected by updating the effective date above. Continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
